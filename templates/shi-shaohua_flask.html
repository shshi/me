BIOGRAPHY

Moving on all the way.

I am from Gansu province, western China. As a child back in hometown, I was always facinated by all kinds of insects and dived into books full of colorful pictures of animals and plants. I liked biology thing.

By high school, I constantly got higher score in biology and physics course. As time went by, the dream of being a scientist in the field of bioscience became more and more clear. Eventually, I chose pharmacology as my major in university.

After graduation, I continued master's degree program in pharmacology at Wenzhou Medical University, a university with high quality in scientific research. As a student with passion on computer science no lower than bioscience, I took three years study & research combined network pharmacology & drug virtual screen with pharmacological experiments. As a result, I pubulished four academic articles and was successfully awarded master's degree in pharmacology.

Prior to working here in Dali University, I applied to be a vulunteer Chinese teacher at Cunfucius Institute in Thailand for two years, followed by another two-year pharmacology teaching at Burapha University.

I currently reside in Dali city and work in Dali University. To continue my childhood dream, I've been looking for the further study for Ph.D. program while working in Dali. I know I have enough passion and creativity to complete my dream.
